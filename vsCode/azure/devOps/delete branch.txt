Delete Branch
Note:
  -This will not delete the copy of the branch on the server. So, delete that via the browser. 
Main Success Scenario:
  1. Click "Source Control" button on left side bar
  2. Checkout the branch to be deleted
  3. See branch name to right of repo name
  4. Click ellipses button, choose "Branch", choose "Delete Branch..."
  5. The non-checked out branches on your local pc are shown in a drop down menu
  6. Choose the branch to delete and click "enter" button on your keyboard
  7. Copy will be deleted only on your local pc
